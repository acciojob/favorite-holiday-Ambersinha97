Dal Lake, J&K

Dal Lake is a beautiful and iconic destination located in Srinagar, the summer capital of the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. Dal Lake is known for its serene waters, floating gardens, houseboats, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
